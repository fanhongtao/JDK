Live Feedback ImageMap (Hints)

  1. My face is surrounded by a 3-D button which takes you to the Java home page when you click on it.
  2. My mouth is surrounded by a rectangle which highlights and invokes two actions when you enter it:
    1. It prints the word "Hi!" in the status line.
    2. It plays an audio file of me saying "Hi".
  3. My left eye is surrounded by a rectangle which highlights and prints the words "That's my left eye" when you enter it.
  4. My right eye is surrounded by a rectangle which highlights and prints the words "That's my right eye" when you enter it.
  5. The triangle banner in the background hanging to the left of my head as you face the picture (on my right in the picture) is surrounded by a 3-D button which takes you to the Java home page when you click on it.
  6. The bright spot to the right of my head (on my left in the picture) highlights and invokes two actions when you enter it:
    1. It prints the word "Chirp!" in the status line.
    2. It plays a chirp sound.
  7. Finally, the entire image provides coordinate feedback if you press, drag, and release any mouse button over it.
